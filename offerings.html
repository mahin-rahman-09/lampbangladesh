Commercial & Industrial Solar

Energy costs are one of the largest and most controllable line items in any Bangladesh business. Our commercial systems are engineered to maximise generation during peak tariff hours, delivering the fastest possible payback.

  • Systems from 20 kW to 5 MW for commercial and industrial facilities

Energy Independence

Battery Storage & Hybrid Systems

For facilities that cannot tolerate power interruptions — hospitals, data centres, cold chains, and precision manufacturing — our hybrid storage systems provide seamless backup with zero transfer time.

  • LiFePO₄ battery storage from 10 kWh to multi-MWh scale
  • Seamless switchover — critical loads never see a power cut
  • Intelligent energy management system (EMS) included
  • 10-year battery warranty with capacity guarantee
  • Compatible with existing diesel gensets as tertiary backup
