What We Do

Complete Solar Solutions,
One Trusted Partner

From rooftop systems to industrial-scale microgrids, we handle every aspect of your solar journey.

Residential Solar

Custom rooftop systems sized to your home's energy needs. Includes net metering setup, inverter installation, and 5-year maintenance coverage.

Commercial Solar

High-yield grid-tied and hybrid systems for offices, shopping centres, hospitals, and educational institutions — with full ROI analysis before you commit.

Industrial Solar

Multi-megawatt EPC projects for factories, RMG facilities, and export processing zones — with demand-side energy management and real-time monitoring.

Battery Storage

Lithium-ion energy storage systems that keep critical loads running through load-shedding periods, reducing dependence on the national grid.

Smart Inverters

Wi-Fi-enabled inverters with remote monitoring, automatic fault detection, and real-time performance dashboards accessible from your phone.

O&M Services

Ongoing operations and maintenance contracts with scheduled panel cleaning, inverter health checks, and a dedicated 24/7 support helpline.
