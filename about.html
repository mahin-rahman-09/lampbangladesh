Our Story

From a Small Team with a Big Conviction

Lamp Bangladesh was founded by engineers who had seen firsthand how load-shedding was costing Bangladesh's businesses billions of taka every year — and how solar technology had reached the point where it could genuinely solve that problem at competitive cost.

Over the years we've grown from a handful of residential projects to a full-service EPC and O&M company serving clients across Dhaka, Chittagong, Gazipur, and beyond. Our portfolio spans RMG factories, private hospitals, university campuses, and thousands of homes.

What hasn't changed is our founding principle: every project we take on should make economic and environmental sense for the customer. We won't sell you a system that doesn't pencil out.

What We Stand For

Three Commitments We
Never Compromise On

01

Technical Honesty

We give you conservative, realistic yield estimates based on your actual roof geometry, local irradiation data, and shading conditions. Overpromised numbers hurt you long-term — and damage trust. We'd rather under-promise and over-deliver.

02

Quality Without Shortcut

We only install Tier 1 panels from manufacturers with bankable warranties. Our mounting structures meet wind-load requirements for Bangladesh's cyclone risk zones. There are cheaper options. There aren't better ones.

03

Lifetime Partnership

A solar system should last 25 years. We structure our O&M contracts so we have a financial incentive to keep your system performing at peak. If performance drops, we fix it — at our cost, not yours.
